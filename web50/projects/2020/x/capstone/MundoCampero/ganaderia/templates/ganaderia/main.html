About Mundo Campero

Mundo Campero is a comprehensive platform that combines various agricultural activities into one powerful application. Our mission is to revolutionize the way farmers manage their operations, providing cutting-edge tools for livestock management, crop cultivation, agricultural marketplace, and rural employment opportunities.

With the integration of artificial intelligence, we aim to optimize processes and become the number one application in Uruguay for agricultural activities and the rural job market.

The Pillars of Mundo Campero

Livestock Management

Comprehensive tools for managing sheep, cattle, and pigs. Currently featuring TuOvino for sheep management with data analysis and pedigree tracking.

Agriculture

Advanced tools for crop management, planting strategies, and process optimization. Future AI integration for crop analysis.

Rural Marketplace

Upcoming platform for farmers to directly connect with consumers and businesses, revolutionizing agricultural product sales.

Rural Job Board

Future efficient job marketplace connecting farm owners with skilled workers in the agricultural sector.

Mundo Campero Roadmap

Phase 1: Sheep Management Current

TuOvino: Comprehensive sheep management system with data analysis and genealogy for pedigree sheep. Explore TuOvino

Phase 2: Expanding Livestock Management

Introducing cattle management tools, similar to TuOvino but tailored for bovine operations.

Phase 3: Agriculture Module

Implementing crop management features, including planting schedules, yield analysis, and potential AI integration for crop health monitoring.

Phase 4: Rural Marketplace

Launching a platform for buying and selling agricultural products, connecting farmers directly with consumers and businesses.

Phase 5: Rural Job Board

Introducing an efficient job marketplace for the agricultural sector, helping farm owners find skilled workers.
